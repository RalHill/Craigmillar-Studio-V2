What is AI automation, exactly?

It's software that does repetitive jobs automatically — without you lifting a finger. When someone calls and you don't answer, it texts them back in 60 seconds. When you finish a job, it sends a Google review request automatically. When someone fills out your form at 11pm, it replies immediately.

You set it up once. Then it runs forever. No staff. No checking. No chasing.
